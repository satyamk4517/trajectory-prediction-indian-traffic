Dataset notes: NGSIM (I-101 freeway) is homogeneous (Cars only), lane-disciplined, units originally in feet — converted to meters via ×0.3048. Warangal (drone-extracted, VID_20231013) is heterogeneous urban with 4 vehicle classes and no lane discipline, inherently a harder prediction task. Metrics are therefore not directly comparable across datasets — they serve as independent baselines within each traffic regime.

Metric notes: NGSIM Phase 1 models report standard ADE/FDE (single prediction). SP-GAT Phases 19–22 report minADE/minFDE over K=3 modes (Winner-Takes-All). Early Warangal models (P16 ID-fix, P17, P18) also report single-mode ADE. "—" = metric not recorded for that run. ★ = best result ever for that class.
